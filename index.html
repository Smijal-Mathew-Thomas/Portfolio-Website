01 Profile

From signals to security.

I started in electronics engineering, learning how systems behave at the level of signals, circuits and protocols. That foundation shapes how I approach defense today: I read a network the way I once read a schematic, tracing cause to effect before reaching for a tool.

My focus is the security of industrial control systems, the networks around them and the day-to-day work of a SOC. I’m actively building depth in cloud security and identity & access. I completed SAIT's Cybersecurity for Control Systems program with honours, monitored live SIEM environments at Cartel Software and now design Capture-the-Flag challenges that put real-world attack scenarios in front of other students.

I'm drawn to problems where the answer isn't obvious. I'd rather understand why an alert fired than memorize a runbook and I bring that same first-principles habit to CTFs, lab builds and automation work.
